Submissions

Guidelines

  • releases of all qualities are allowed
  • The majority of your release must fall under experimental electronic which includes anything from noise and ambient to breakcore and lolicore
  • Direct references to pedophilia and other illegal content is allowed but no actual illegal content such as child porn is allowed in audio or images (This doesn't include copyrighted material. Go nuts!)
  • No biggoted material. If you have to ask then it probably is
  • We will never charge for a digital release. People may donate to you if they want to. 100% minus Paypal fees goes to you!

If you want us to permiere your release in our streaming setup, just ask! If you have any other requests about your release, just ask!


Physicals

  • We do cassettes and CDRs
  • We do have quality limits for physical releases
  • How many cassettes is dependent on the quality of the release and your popularity
  • 80% of the physical's profits go to you
  • We will set the price of the physical release and use a 3rd party payment processor
  • Paypal or crypto for artists only!!
